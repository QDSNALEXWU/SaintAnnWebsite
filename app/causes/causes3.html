Performing arts (traditional music, dance and theatre).

Description

Our mission is to inspire curiosity, creativity and passion within child of all ages while providing the highest quality professional training in the musical theatre arts. The arts are a function of being human, as they have been in every culture across time. Performing arts training at Saint Ann Foundation Centre cultivates children’s’ ability to create and empowers them to use their voice with courage.
Performing Arts develops the critical problem solving and communication skills of children that are vital in creating today’s complex society. Children of Saint Ann Foundation performing arts education will be innovative in their approach to contemporary society’s most pressing issues, as they become savvy consumers and producers of culture.
